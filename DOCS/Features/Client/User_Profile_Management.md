# User Profile Management

User profile management is a core feature of the client system in the Helix Discord bot. It allows users to manage their personal information, preferences, and account settings. The user profile serves as a centralized hub for users to customize their experience and tailor it to their specific needs.

## Key Features

1. **Profile Information**: Users can view and edit their profile information, such as username, avatar, bio, location, and other relevant details. This information helps create a personalized and engaging experience within the Helix Discord bot ecosystem.

2. **Linked Accounts**: Users can link their Discord account with other external accounts or services, such as Google, Twitch, Reddit, Stack Overflow, GitHub, YouTube, and email. This integration enables seamless interactions and enhances the functionality and features available to the user.

3. **Password Management**: Users have the ability to change their account password. This feature ensures account security and allows users to update their credentials periodically for added protection.

4. **Email Management**: Users can manage their email address associated with their account. This includes updating the email address, verifying email addresses, and receiving important account-related notifications.

5. **Privacy Settings**: The user profile management provides options for users to control their privacy settings. Users can specify their preferred privacy level for profile visibility, managing who can view their information and engage with them.

6. **Preferences and Settings**: Users can configure their preferences and settings to customize their experience within the Helix Discord bot ecosystem. This may include notification settings, theme selection, language preferences, and other configurable options.

7. **Account Deletion**: Users have the ability to delete their account if they no longer wish to use the Helix Discord bot. This feature ensures users have control over their data and can exercise their right to be forgotten.
