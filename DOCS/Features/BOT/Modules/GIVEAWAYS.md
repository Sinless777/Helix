# Giveaways

The Giveaways module enables users to create and manage giveaways within your Discord server. Giveaways are a popular way to engage your community and reward members with exciting prizes. This module provides the necessary functionality to organize and administer giveaways effectively.

## Features

The Giveaways module includes the following features:

- **Creation of Giveaways**: With this module, you can create giveaways with customizable parameters. You can specify the duration of the giveaway, the prize or reward being offered, and any additional requirements for entry. This flexibility allows you to tailor the giveaways to your community's preferences and interests.

- **Automatic Winner Selection**: Once the giveaway duration has ended, the module automatically selects winners based on the specified criteria. This ensures a fair and transparent selection process, enhancing the trust and credibility of the giveaways.

- **Optional Additional Features**: The Giveaways module may offer additional features to enhance the giveaway experience. These features can include options such as multiple winners, role requirements for entry, restrictions on duplicate entries, or advanced customization options for the giveaway format and appearance.

## Required Information

To set up and utilize the Giveaways module, you will need the following information:

- **Giveaway Duration**: Specify the duration of the giveaway, either in the form of a duration (e.g., 1 day, 12 hours) or a specific date and time (datetime).

- **Giveaway Prize or Reward**: Define the prize or reward that participants will have a chance to win. This can be any type of item, virtual currency, role, or other benefits that you wish to offer.

- **Optional: Entry Requirements and Customization**: Decide if you want to set any additional entry requirements, such as being a member of a specific role, reaching a certain level, or fulfilling certain conditions. Additionally, consider any customization options available for the giveaway, such as formatting the announcement message, adding emojis, or providing additional instructions.

Please note that the implementation and configuration details of the Giveaways module can vary depending on the Discord bot you are using. It is recommended to consult the official documentation or support channels of the bot for accurate instructions on how to enable and configure the giveaways.

For further assistance or questions, please refer to the respective module documentation or reach out to the bot's support channels.
